About NECYSC

About Us

Established in 1986, the New England Chinese Youth Summer Camp (NECYSC) is a non-profit organization run by dedicated parents and volunteers. Hosted at Brandeis University, our week-long summer camp is designed for youth aged 6 to 15. A range of programs - including Day, Overnight, and ExplorAsian - are provided, with the primary goal of nurturing cultural appreciation and fostering a strong community spirit.

Our campers actively participate in cultural activities like Chinese Yo-yo, Kung-fu, and Calligraphy throughout the day. These are complemented by counselor-led events such as Carnival, Fun In The Sun, Talent Show, End-of-the-Week Dance, and the eagerly-awaited CIT Dress Up. Campers create life-long memories and friendships that go beyond the ordinary camp setting. As the week concludes, each group performs skills they learned during their time at NECYSC for friends and family members.
